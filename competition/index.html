"Wings are not only for birds; they are also for minds. Human potential stops at some point somewhere beyond infinity"
The Paper presentation contest of EVOKE'19 aims at tapping this infinite potential of the human mind to generate an idea, which when given a proper platform, can transform into a path breaking discovery. We invite you to bring forth such original ideas and present it in a genuine and comprehensive way.
Rules and Regulations:
1. The competition is open to undergraduate students of all disciplines.
2. The Paper should follow a standard IEEE format.
3. Each team may have maximum 4 members.
Submit your entries on or before 10/01/2018
