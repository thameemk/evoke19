Rules:
1. The maximum size of the team should not be more than 5 members.
2. The topic will only be revealed on the day of hackathon.
3. The hack can be software based or hardware based, as long as it is related to the topic.
4. All work on a project should be done at the hackathon. Teams can work on an idea that they have worked on before (as long as they do not re-use code).
5. The time allotted for the hackathon is 24 hours. Teams must stop hacking once the time is up.Â
